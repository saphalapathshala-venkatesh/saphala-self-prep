ADMIN COURSE BUILDER CONTEXT

When creating a course in Admin, two key fields are selected:

1) Product Category (one of the platform product types)

Examples:
- Free Demo Courses & Tests
- Test Series
- Video Courses
- HTML Courses (Ebooks)
- PDF Courses
- Flashcard Courses
- LIVE Classes
- Combo Packages

This determines HOW the course is consumed.

2) Exam Category (called “Category” in Admin)

Examples:
- AP Police
- APPSC Group 1
- APPSC Group 2
- Banking
- SSC

This determines WHO the course is for.
