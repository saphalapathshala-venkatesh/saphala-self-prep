2. Better mobile card height

On mobile:

Every card, regardless of type, must have a small header and small footer.

Approx. 10% of total card height
Must include:

SUBJECT

Total XP

Footer requirements

Approx. 10% of total card height

institute name

tagline

Keep footer subtle and very small, but premium.

1. Info cards

Required flow:

3. Fill in the blanks

Try Again

Flip Card
